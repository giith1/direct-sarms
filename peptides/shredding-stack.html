Product Breakdown

S4 – Andarine

Originally developed for osteoporosis and muscle wasting, S4 binds selectively to androgen receptors in bone and muscle. It promotes gene expression changes that support protein synthesis and anabolic activity.

Clinical comparisons place it shoulder to shoulder with Stanazole—minus the same level of hormonal suppression. Ideal during calorie-cutting phases to retain muscle mass while torching fat.

  • Formula: C₁₉H₁₈F₃N₃O₆

  • Weight: 441.4 g/mol

MK-2866 – Ostarine

Think of Ostarine as the disciplined, efficient lab assistant—directly targeting muscle and bone androgen receptors without hormonal side effects.

Lab studies show significant increases in lean mass, bone density, and recovery time post-exercise in test animals. The takeaway? Ostarine builds without the bloat.

  • Formula: C₁₉H₁₄F₃N₃O₃

  • Weight: 389.33 g/mol

GW-501516 – Cardarine

Despite common belief, Cardarine isn’t a SARM. It's a PPARδ agonist that enhances fatty acid metabolism and increases endurance—even at rest.

It's like flipping your metabolism’s switch to ""turbo mode."" That’s why it’s a go-to in the research community for cutting cycles and endurance studies.

  • Formula: C₂₁H₁₈F₃NO₃S₂

  • Weight: 453.498 g/mol

SR-9009 – Stenabolic

SR-9009 is the metabolic maestro. By targeting Rev-ErBα, it regulates the body’s circadian rhythm, energy output, and fat storage.
