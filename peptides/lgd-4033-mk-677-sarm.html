MK-677 (Ibutamoren): Results

Though not a SARM in the traditional sense, MK-677 (Ibutamoren) is a non-peptide selective agonist of the ghrelin receptor and a powerful growth hormone secretagogue. Unlike typical growth hormone boosters, MK-677 stimulates the pituitary gland to naturally increase growth hormone levels without affecting cortisol or other hormones.

MK-677 has been shown to help with muscle growth, fat loss, and bone density improvements. It's particularly noted for supporting recovery, increasing lean muscle mass, and boosting performance without the undesirable side effects often seen with traditional growth hormone therapies. However, it's important to note that MK-677 has not yet received FDA approval for human consumption.
